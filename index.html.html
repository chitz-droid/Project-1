What is Timey

The physical anchor for your digital flow state.

Timey is a compact, tactile keypad designed to make time-tracking effortless. Stop fumbling through apps and tab-switching — just press a key and your timer starts.

Built for creators, developers, and deep-work practitioners who believe that how you manage time defines how you live. Timey sits on your desk as a silent, glowing reminder that your time is your most valuable resource.
